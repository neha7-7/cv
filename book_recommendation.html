Discover books with me!

Here are some of the books that I enjoyed reading and I hope you would like it too.

1.I am watching you

Book by Teresa driscoll

I Am Watching You by Teresa Driscoll is an intriguing thriller. Though the premise is simple,
the way it's told is interesting.This is a debut novel by the writer and it's quite a sparkling start.
The book does well to maintain an even pace - though it's not exactly a fast paced crime thriller.
The element of suspense - that is what defines a good mystery book - has been well created and it hangs
in throughout the novel till its end.

2.You are a badass

Book by Jen Sincero

The #1 New York Times Bestseller You Are A Badass is the self-help book for people who desperately want to
improve their lives but don't want to get busted doing it.By the end of You Are a Badass, you'll understand
why you are how you are, how to love what you can't change, how to change what you don't love, and how to use
The Force to kick some serious ass.

3.The alchemist

By Paulo Coelho

Paulo Coelho's masterpiece tells the magical story of Santiago, an Andalusian shepherd boy who yearns to travel in
search of a worldly treasure as extravagant as any ever found.The story of the treasures Santiago finds along the way
teaches us, as only a few stories can, about the essential wisdom of listening to our hearts, learning to read the omens
strewn along life's path, and, above all, following our dreams.

4.The subtle art of not giving a f

By Mark Manson

In this generation-defining self-help guide, a superstar blogger cuts through the crap to show us how to stop trying to be positive
all the time so that we can truly become better, happier people.For decades we've been told that positive thinking is the key to a
happy, rich life. "F*ck positivity," Mark Manson says. "Let's be honest, shit is f*cked, and we have to live with it.
